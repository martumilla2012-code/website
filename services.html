Our Cleaning Services

What’s Included in Our Services

We provide flexible cleaning options to fit your schedule, lifestyle, and needs. Whether you need regular maintenance or a one-time deep clean, we’ve got you covered.

Deep Cleaning

A detailed top-to-bottom cleaning that focuses on hard-to-reach areas, built-up dirt, and neglected spaces. Ideal for first-time clients or seasonal refreshes.

Regular Weekly Cleaning

Perfect for maintaining a consistently clean home. This service helps keep your space fresh and organized every week with routine cleaning tasks.

Bi-Weekly Cleaning

A popular option for clients who want a balance between affordability and cleanliness. Every two weeks, we refresh and maintain your home.

Monthly Cleaning

Ideal for lighter maintenance. This service helps keep your home clean on a monthly schedule, focusing on essential areas and upkeep.

Move-In / Move-Out Cleaning

Designed for moving situations, this service ensures the property is spotless before moving in or after moving out, including detailed cleaning of all rooms.

Post-Construction Cleaning

A specialized service that removes dust, debris, and leftover construction materials after renovations or building projects, leaving the space clean and ready to use.

Seasonal Cleaning & Add-On Services

We also offer additional services for clients who need extra help beyond standard cleaning packages. These services must be requested in advance and may include an additional fee.

  • Cleaning inside refrigerator and/or oven
  • Deep oven cleaning with specialized products
  • Wiping cabinets, drawers, and shelving
  • Closet and storage area cleaning
  • Patio and outdoor furniture cleaning
  • Interior window washing
  • Whole-home sanitizing using professional methods
  • Garage sweeping and dust removal

These add-on services are not included in standard cleaning packages and should be scheduled in advance during booking.

Services Not Included

To maintain safety, quality, and consistency, there are certain tasks that we do not provide.

  • Carpet, upholstery, or curtain shampooing
  • Heavy junk or debris removal
  • Wall scrubbing or paint removal
  • Floor stripping, waxing, or restoration
  • Laundry services (washing, folding, ironing)
  • Chandelier or high-risk fixture cleaning
  • Hoarding or biohazard cleanup
  • Restoration from fire, smoke, or water damage

Service Terms & Expectations

We are committed to delivering high-quality results and clear communication with every service we provide.

For fixed-price or recurring cleanings, clients are encouraged to review the completed work and contact us within 48 hours if any issues are found. We will gladly return to address any concerns related to the original service.

For hourly-based cleanings, the scope of work depends on the time reserved. If additional time is needed, clients will always be given the option to approve it before work continues.

Once a service is completed and approved by the client, it is considered accepted as delivered. Instead of refunds, we focus on resolving concerns through follow-up cleaning when applicable.
