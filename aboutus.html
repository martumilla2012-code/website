About Us

House Cleaning You Can Depend On

Get the cleaning you need, when you need it

The Cleaning Girl LLC proudly serves Richmond and surrounding areas with high-quality residential and commercial cleaning services at affordable rates. For over 25 years, we have been dedicated to helping families and businesses maintain clean, healthy, and comfortable spaces.

What started as a small family-run service has grown into a trusted local company built on reliability, consistency, and care. From the beginning, our mission has been simple — treat every home and every client with respect, honesty, and attention to detail.

Today, our goal remains the same: to deliver exceptional cleaning services that allow our clients to enjoy stress-free, refreshed, and welcoming environments. We take pride in every space we clean, no matter the size or condition.

Your Trusted Cleaning Professionals in Richmond

We are committed to maintaining the highest standards in the cleaning industry. Whether you need a one-time deep clean or ongoing service, our team ensures every job is completed with care, precision, and professionalism.

All members of our team are carefully trained and selected to ensure quality and trust. We focus on reliability, attention to detail, and customer satisfaction in every visit.
